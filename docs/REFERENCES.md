# References

Core references you can use while implementing equations:

- Bate, Mueller, White, *Fundamentals of Astrodynamics*.
- Curtis, *Orbital Mechanics for Engineering Students*.
- Vallado, *Fundamentals of Astrodynamics and Applications*.
- NASA Glenn: rocket equations and propulsion basics.
- FAR and MechJeb source/docs for integration behavior.

Suggested workflow:

1. Implement the equation from textbook form in `KspMathCore`.
2. Compare outputs with a hand-calculated sample.
3. Add test vectors from your own mission scenarios.
